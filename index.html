About Teatime

Qualities

We use only the finest quality tea leaves from the best tea gardens in the world. Our teas are carefully processed and stored to preserve their flavor and aroma.

Opportunities

We are open to partnerships with other tea companies, restaurants, cafes, or health food stores. We believe that by working together, we can reach a wider audience and promote the benefits of tea.

Marketplaces

We have more than 200 stores in all major cities and towns in Indonesia. We reach our customers online through our website, social media, and several online marketplaces.

Community
